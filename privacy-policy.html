Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

KCalTrack ("the app", "we") is a calorie and nutrition tracking app developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data stored on your device

All meal logs, nutrition goals, and personal profile information (age, height, weight, activity level) are stored only on your device. This data is never uploaded to any server by us and never shared with third parties. You can export or delete this data at any time from within the app.

2. Permissions the app requests

  • Camera — used to scan barcodes on food packaging and to capture photos of meals for AI-assisted nutritional analysis. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Microphone — used to allow voice input when describing a meal to the AI assistant. Audio is processed locally or via the AI service described below and is not recorded or retained.
  • Read media images — used to let you select an existing photo from your gallery for meal analysis instead of taking a new photo.
  • Internet — required for AI meal analysis (Firebase AI / Gemini), product barcode lookups (Open Food Facts), and to serve ads (Google AdMob).

3. AI-assisted meal analysis (Firebase AI / Gemini)

When you use the AI chat feature, the text you type and any photo you choose to share are sent to Google's Gemini API via Firebase AI Logic. This data is processed to generate nutritional estimates. It is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not store, log, or have access to the content of these requests. AI-generated results are estimates only — always verify with a qualified nutrition professional.

If you prefer not to send any data to an external AI service, you can switch to the on-device AI provider (Gemma 4, runs fully offline) in Settings → AI Provider.

4. User-supplied API keys

As an alternative to Firebase AI, you may optionally enter your own API keys for third-party AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini) in Settings → AI Provider. If you do:

  • Keys are stored only on your device using Android's encrypted storage (EncryptedSharedPreferences). We never see, transmit, or have access to your keys.
  • When you use the AI chat feature, your meal descriptions and any photos you share are sent directly from your device to the API provider you selected (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google). This data is subject to that provider's own privacy policy:
    • OpenAI Privacy Policy
    • Anthropic Privacy Policy
    • Google Privacy Policy
  • You can remove your stored keys at any time from Settings → AI Provider.

5. Firebase Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised, aggregated usage data (e.g. which screens are opened, feature interaction counts). This data helps improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included. You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free tier of the app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads via your device's advertising settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads on Android). For more information see Google's advertising policy.

Users who purchase the Premium subscription receive an ad-free experience and no AdMob data is collected for those sessions.

7. Barcode product lookups

When you scan a barcode, the app queries the Open Food Facts public database to retrieve nutritional information. Only the barcode number is sent; no personal data is transmitted. Open Food Facts is an open-data project — see their privacy policy.

8. No account or sign-in required

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in. Your identity is never collected by us.

9. Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository or contact the developer directly through the Play Store listing.

KCalTrack is an independent app not affiliated with Google, Anthropic, or OpenAI.
